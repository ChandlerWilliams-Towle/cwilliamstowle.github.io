Services

Service What it is
Turnkey projects This services has us working on the project from conception to completion. We deliver rennovations on a turnkey basis. We streamline the process for tight deadlines and when resources are limited.
New Construction We also work on the basis of independent designers if needed. We have staff and designers on hand if more comprehensive design-build services are required.
Interior fit-outs A rather simple service of building out a space that is leased to a tenant. This includes walls and functioning utlities among other things. We have skilled craftsman to ensure the fit-out is completed fully before the tenant arrives.
Site Improvements Site improvements are as simple as the name suggests. Here we improve the site in ways that accommodate or contribute to the structure. This includes parking, shelter, and site lighting.
Taking over underway projects Our company offers to take over existing projects that have gone off track. Costs will be discussed with the client.

Estimating Costs

How we come to figure out the overall price of a project is simple. We will first come to the location or site of the project with our client to review it for safety concerns and check with the client on their budget. This is critical to make sure we don't make a grandiose project outside of their price range. We then consult with our expert engineers to find materials that give the client the project they want, all within costs. We finalize this with a bid sent to the client for them to check and give the okay to start work.
